Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact Lakbay Rent for a faster response?

For immediate assistance, you may contact us through our official hotline at +63 976 423 3488 or send us a message via Facebook Messenger. You may also email us at support@lakbayrent.com for inquiries and concerns. Our customer service team is available Monday to Sunday, from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

What payment method do you accept?

At present, Lakbay Rent accepts payments exclusively through GCash. Upon confirmation of your booking, our official GCash account details will be provided. Kindly ensure that proof of payment is sent to our team to validate your reservation.

How do I book a car?

To book a vehicle, log in to your Lakbay Rent account, browse the available cars, select your preferred unit and rental date, and confirm your booking. A confirmation message and booking reference will be issued once payment has been verified.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes. Clients may cancel or modify their bookings at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup. Requests made beyond this period may be subject to a modification or cancellation fee, depending on the terms of the booking.

What should I do in case of an emergency or accident?

In the event of an emergency or accident, please contact our emergency hotline (+63 976 423 3488) immediately. Our support team will assist you in coordinating with the authorities and arranging the necessary vehicle assistance.
